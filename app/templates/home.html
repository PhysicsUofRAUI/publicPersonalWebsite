Bin Temperature Sensor

As of right now farmers have two choices when it comes to grain monitoring either pay several thousand dollars per bin to have a computer monitor their grain or monitor it themselves. Monitoring themselves requires time and it is not that accurate so many times the farmer will end up with spoiled grain from the grain heating.

It occured to me that the system that farmers can buy could be made with open source hardware platforms such as Arduino or Raspberry pi. I have set out to design these systems using open source hardware and then publish my results. During the December 2019, January and Feburary 2020 I made significant progress and even had a prototype running inside a empty bin. After that progress was made I slowed down, but now I intend to ramp back up on that proejct. See the blog for more recent updates and below for some relevant outside links!
